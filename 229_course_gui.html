229th Information Ops Sqdn Course GUI

A redevelopment of the course GUI for the VT Air Guard 229th Information Ops Sqdn, which taught cyber and informations ops courses to members of various military and civillian organizations as prerequisites to more advanced in-resident training. The goal was to rebuild its existing non-mobile friendly interface relying on Flash components to a fully responsive HTML & CSS interface. In addition to the interface containing the reading material of the course, I developed various interactive learning tools, such as ungraded quizes in the form of multiple choice questions and drag &, drop word-matching, and a command-line demo which serve to keep students engaged and break up the monotony of the course.
